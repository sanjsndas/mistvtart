cookies · clarity

We use cookies to enhance your experience, not to track you. This policy explains what we store and how you can manage your preferences.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They help us remember your preferences, improve site performance, and understand how our content is used. We use only essential and functional cookies — no invasive tracking.

Your control

You can manage or disable cookies at any time via your browser settings. However, disabling essential cookies may affect the functionality of our site. We respect your choice and will never set cookies without your explicit consent.
