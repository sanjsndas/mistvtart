    • privacy · reverence

    Your data is treated with the same care we give to words. This policy outlines what we collect, why, and how we protect it.

    1. Information we collect

    We collect minimal, essential data to maintain the integrity of our correspondence and improve the reading experience. This includes:

    • Contact details — name, email address, and message content when you use our contact form.
    • Usage data — anonymised analytics (page views, referral sources) to understand readership patterns.
    • Cookies — only functional and preference cookies; we never sell or misuse tracking data.

    2. How we use your information

    We use your data solely for the purposes you expect:

    • To respond to inquiries, pitches, or feedback.
    • To send occasional newsletters (only if you explicitly opt in).
    • To improve our content and user experience through aggregated analytics.

    ✧ pledge: We never share, sell, or rent your personal data to third parties. Your trust is our currency.

    3. Data storage & security

    All data is stored on secure servers with industry-standard encryption. We retain contact form submissions for a maximum of 24 months, after which they are securely deleted. You may request deletion or correction at any time.

    4. Your rights

    You have the right to access, amend, or delete your personal data. For any request, please contact us via the details below. We will respond within 30 days.

    • Right to access — receive a copy of the data we hold.
    • Right to rectification — correct inaccurate or incomplete data.
    • Right to erasure — request deletion of your data.

    5. Third-party links

    Our site may contain links to external platforms (e.g., social media, bookstores). We are not responsible for their privacy practices. We encourage you to read their policies before engaging.

    6. Updates to this policy

    We may update this policy to reflect changes in law or our practices. The latest version will always be published on this page, with the effective date noted.
